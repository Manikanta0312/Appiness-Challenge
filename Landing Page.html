What is IVUS(Intravascular Ultrasound) and why is it better?

Intravascular Ultrasound(IVUS) allows the doctor to see a coronary artery inside out, which provides real time information that goes beyond routine imaging methods. Vulnerable plaque, which exists inside the arternal wall, also can be visualized by IVUS thereby preventing rupture and potential heart attacks.

Benefits

Increased accuracy of stent placement
Faster recovery
Real-time evaluation
Better quality of life
Reduced complications
